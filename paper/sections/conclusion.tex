This paper studies small underwater biological object detection in low-visibility scenes
from a raw-versus-enhanced dual-domain perspective. The proposed QDCR-Net combines
bidirectional cross-residual interaction, quality-aware dynamic fusion, and a
small-object-oriented neck to improve robustness under underwater degradation. The next
stage of the project is to complete the implementation, run controlled experiments on
URPC2018 and RUOD, and validate the method through ablation and robustness analysis.
